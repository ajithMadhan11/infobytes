About Us

"Great things are done by a series of small things brought together".

InfoBytes is a Tech club of Department of Information Technology with a community of students highly enthusiastic about coding, ethical hacking, Web Designing and all other aspects of programming. We organize activities related to Development (Web Development, Open Source, Game/App Development), programming contests, hackathons. Lecture series and Webnier are regularly taken on algorithms, web hijacking, development, among other things.

Our Activities

We organize activities related to Development (Web Development, Open Source, Game/App Development), programming contests, hackathons. Lecture series and Webnier are regularly taken on algorithms, web hijacking, development, among other things.

Mission & Vision

To empower the students of KARE through competitive programming skills and technology. To Challenge all individuals to achieve above their highest expectations in programming platform. Our mission is to strengthen the programming skills of the students in the department.

Our Goals

  • Take part in competitions like ACM-ICPC, Google Code Jam,GSOC
  • Daily coding practice between 4-5pm after academics hours.
  • Conduct coding competitions to reach every student on campus
  • Conduct workshops, Webinars , Guest Lectures
